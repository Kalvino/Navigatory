Description

Monument Valley's name comes from his particular rocky monuments. Indeed, the wind has eroded this plateau of Colorado over time and now only a few rocks are left, witness of the last centuries. According to the sun's position, this orange-colored rocky peaks (whom the most known are "the grand Indian chief", "the three sisters" and "The cow-boy's boot") change into a powerful ochre, showing to the visitor a shifting sight. By visiting and walking on the site, you will appreciate the immensity of this plain and the majesty of his monuments. However, you will not be the first ones: from Steven Spielberg to Stanley Kubrick, a lot of moviemakers and artists have found their inspiration there... It's your turn now.

Activities

In-car tour

The visitors drive on a 27km long stony way with their own vehicle. But the rocker panel should be high enough (the road is available for all kinds of cars provided that it's not been raining). This drive will walk you through the most impressive monuments and a series of wonderful.

Jeep tour with Navajo guide

If you don't want to drive with your own car in the park, you can embark in the jeep of a Navajo guide who will make the visit for you. Please go to the visitor center for more information. Some tours offer the possibility to discover the southern part of the park, which is only available with a guide ($65, 2hours 30).

Horse-riding with a Navajo guide

On horseback, you will peacefully discover the park and have the feeling that you are living in a Western movie. Different rides are possible for all levels (including beginners). You will find an information point in the car park ($79-$100, 1 hour 30-2 hours 30).
